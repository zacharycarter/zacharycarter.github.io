About

I'm thirty-three years old, and I live as well as work in Northern Virginia.

Virginia

I was born and raised by my loving parents in the Northern Virginia area. I graduated from Oakton High School, and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in history from Virginia Commonwealth University.

Software Development

I started creating websites when I was in middle school. I began by learning HTML, then JavaScript, and eventually CSS. In high school, I learned the C programming language as well as some rudimentary C++.

Gaming

I grew up around video games, starting with an Atari 2600 console. Eventually I moved onto various Nintendo, Sega, Sony, and Microsoft consoles, however my ultimate landing spot, was the PC.

Sports

During my childhood and teenage years, I played quite a few sports. Game Development

During high school, and after graduating from university, I began studying how video games are made. I started off by building Roguelike games in C++ and Java, placing an emphasis on Procedural Content Generation. Eventually I came across the Nim programming language. I've used it to develop my own Game Development Libraries as well as learn more about Game Engine Development.
